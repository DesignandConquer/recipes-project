Fried Chicken Cesar Salad Wrap

Home Page

Great for a picnic at the park, beach, or in bed. This wrap can do it all.

The council rated this dish was rated a "delightfully crunchy, 31.379" on the gross to scrumptious scale.

Ingredients

  • Almond flour
  • Chicken
  • Bread crumbs
  • Tortillas/wraps
  • Croutons
  • Eggs
  • Seasonings to your liking
  • Lettuce
  • Cheese
  • Cesar salad dressing (Chef Felix uses the one from Trader Joe's)

Steps

  1. Beat (tenderize) your chicken vigorously in parchment paper
  2. Create your stations:
    • In a bowl wisk your eggs for the egg wash (season if you'd like)
    • In another bowl, pour a generous amound of almond flour, bread crumbs and cheese
  3. Bathe your chicken in the egg wash, then in the second bowl cover in the flour, cruton, and cheese mix.
  4. Top with extra cheese if you're feeling cheesy
  5. Drizzle oil on your battered chicken, and air fry at 400F for 15-20 minutes or until golden brown
  6. Once the chicken is done, combine the rest of your ingredients in your wrap
  7. In a pan, quickly sear the wraps to get a good seal
  8. Enjoy now, later, or give it to a friend! (but make one for yourself too, they're good)

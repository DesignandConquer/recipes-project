Hot Honey Salmon

Home Page

This is a simple, delicious salmon dish that's easy to make, but won't take all night. It's hot, it's sweet, it's a tasty treat. What more could you want?

The council rated this dish a 17.95 on the gross to scrumptios scale, so it's a must try if you've got what it takes.

Ingredients

  • Salmon
  • Tamare
  • Chili paste
  • Hot Honey
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Green beans
  • Rice
  • Cilantro (if you're feeling fancy)

Steps

  1. Make the marinade. In a bowl, combine the:
    • Tamare
    • Chili paste
    • A generous amount of hot honey
    • Garlic
    • Ginger
  2. Cook the salmon in the air fryer at 400F for 15ish minutes
  3. While salmon is cooking, prepare your rice and green beans in your favorite way
  4. When everything is done, serve the salmon hot on a bed of rice and green beans. Top with the marinade and cilantro, and dig in!
